The Adventures of Leo the Lion: Right to Education

Panel 1: Leo the Lion, a young and curious lion, is walking towards the school gate but stops and looks confused. He says, "Wait, lions can't go to school, can they?"

Panel 2: His best friend, Gigi the Giraffe, comes running to him. "Of course, you can, Leo! Every child has the right to education, even lions!" she says.

Panel 3: Leo gets excited and says, "So I can learn math and science, too?" Gigi nods enthusiastically, "Absolutely! And don't forget art and music!"

Panel 4: The next day, Leo and Gigi are in class, and Leo is trying to solve a simple math problem. "Hmm... I think the answer is... 25?" Leo says.

Panel 5: The teacher, Mrs. Owl, a wise old bird, flies over. "Great job, Leo! You're learning quickly. Remember, the right to education is a superpower!"

Panel 6: Leo smiles and says, "I'm glad I didn't let my doubts stop me. Education is for everyone, and now I know my right to it!"
