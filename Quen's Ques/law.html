Fundamental Laws for Children

Law Number Law Title Description
1 Right to Education Every child has the right to free and compulsory education.
2 Right to Protection from Abuse Children must be protected from all forms of abuse, neglect, and exploitation.
3 Right to Participation Children have the right to express their opinions and have them taken into account in matters affecting them.
4 Right to Health and Safety Children are entitled to the highest standard of health and access to necessary medical services.
5 Right to Family Life Children should be cared for by their parents and live in a family environment whenever possible.
